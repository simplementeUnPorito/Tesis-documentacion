\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

This exploratory two-board proof of concept shows that sequential four-VDAC foreground calibration, with codes frozen during acquisition, substantially lowers the residual DC offsets across the conditioning stages---most consequentially at the LP output delivered to the acquisition ADC---relative to a shared fixed-reference baseline (Section~\ref{sec:experimental-validation}). The evidence is descriptive feasibility, not population-level validation; the 10--50~m field record confirms integration, not comparative performance. The studies required to establish generality are outlined in Section~\ref{sec:discussion}.
