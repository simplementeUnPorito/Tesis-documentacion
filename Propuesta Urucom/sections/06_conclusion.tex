\section{Conclusion}

In this two-board proof of concept, the ADC-facing LP sample-mean offset was 0.4--3.9\%FS after four-VDAC calibration versus 2.9--45.8\%FS under the shared fixed-reference baseline across eight reported cells, with codes frozen during acquisition. The 10--50~m field record confirms integration, not comparative performance. Future work will expand the independent hardware population, sensitivity and ablation studies, and controlled field evaluation.
